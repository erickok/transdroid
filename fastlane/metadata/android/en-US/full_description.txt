Allows you to manage your torrents. All popular clients are supported:
<ul>
<li>μTorrent</li>
<li>Transmission</li>
<li>RTorrent</li>
<li>BiglyBT and Vuze</li>
<li>Deluge</li>
<li>BitTorrent 6</li>
<li>qBittorrent</li>
</ul>

You can view and manage the running torrents and individual files.
Adding is easy via the integrated search, RSS feeds or the QR code scanner.
Monitor progress with the home screen widget or alarm background service.

See also <a href="https://f-droid.org/packages/org.transdroid.search">Transdroid Torrent Search</a>
and <a href="https://f-droid.org/packages/org.transdroid.lite">Transdrone</a> - a lite version without RSS.
